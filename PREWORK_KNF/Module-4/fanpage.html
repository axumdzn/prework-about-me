Bio

Illenium is an American DJ that focuses his time as an EDM artist. Born Nicholas D. Miller in Chicago, Illinois, he has released four studio albums with his first three being a trilogy. In less that 5 years form his release of his first album he has become someone who can fill venues with huge capacity limits and has the recognition to headline festivals even with other stars attending.
